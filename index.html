About PTSS Coffee

The Campus Cafe by Media Creative Centre at Politeknik Tuanku Syed Sirajuddin (PTSS) is fully integrated into the campus Pauh Inn. It serves as a dual-purpose space offering local student entrepreneurs retail experience while providing a relaxing haven for the student body.

Our Mission

To create a cozy space for students that blends comfortable learning with coffee relaxation.
